Oasis Advocacy, founded by Adv. Shital Patil, is a client-focused legal practice offering over a decade of experience in Real Estate Law, Civil Litigation, and Consumer Disputes. With 10+ years of courtroom practice, document advisory, and client representation, Adv. Patil brings legal expertise grounded in trust, ethics, and results. Her professional journey includes working on over 500 real estate transactions, successfully guiding clients through property registration, title clearance, MOUs, POAs, and sale/purchase agreements. She has effectively represented clients in civil courts, consumer forums, and recovery tribunals across Pune and nearby regions. Adv. Patil’s notable experience includes assisting housing societies in legal formation, securing deemed conveyance, and coordinating with builders and banks for document clearances and compliance. With a strong foundation in legal drafting, litigation, and consultation, Oasis Advocacy is trusted by individuals, developers, and cooperative societies for timely, professional, and personalized legal support.
